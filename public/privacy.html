Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you take the DISC personality assessment on this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

When you complete the DISC assessment, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and role applied for
  • Assessment Responses: Your selections for all 24 question groups (Most/Least choices)
  • Calculated Results: Your DISC scores (D, I, S, C), primary type, and type order
  • Technical Data: Basic submission metadata (timestamp, form submission ID)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Evaluate your suitability for the role you applied for
  • Understand your workplace communication style and preferences
  • Contact you regarding the hiring process
  • Improve our assessment tools and hiring practices
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Netlify Forms, a trusted cloud service provider. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access to assessment results is restricted to authorized hiring team members only
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for hiring and legal compliance purposes

Who Has Access to Your Data

Your assessment data may be accessed by:

  • Authorized members of our hiring and human resources teams
  • Relevant hiring managers for the role you applied for
  • Service providers who help us operate the assessment (Netlify)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your assessment data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw your application and request removal of your data
  • Object: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@example.com.

Cookies and Tracking

This assessment website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not track your browsing behavior or collect analytics data beyond what is necessary to process your assessment submission.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this assessment:

  • Netlify: Web hosting and form data collection (subject to Netlify's Privacy Policy)
  • Tailwind CSS CDN: CSS framework delivery (no personal data collected)

Data Retention

We retain your assessment data for:

  • Active candidates: Duration of the hiring process plus 6 months
  • Hired candidates: As part of employment records per legal requirements
  • Unsuccessful candidates: Up to 12 months for potential future opportunities (with your consent)

You may request earlier deletion at any time by contacting us.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Children's Privacy

This assessment is intended for adults seeking employment. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 16 years of age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Important Note About DISC Assessment

The DISC assessment is a workplace behavioral style indicator, not a medical or psychological diagnosis. Results are used solely for employment evaluation purposes and do not constitute medical or mental health information under HIPAA or similar regulations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@example.com
  • General Inquiries: contact@example.com

Legal Compliance:

This privacy policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and other relevant privacy laws. For jurisdiction-specific rights and provisions, please contact our privacy team.
